I'm a Senior Security Architect at **Baylor Scott & White Health**, one of the largest nonprofit health systems in the US. My focus areas include:

- **Authentication & Identity** — CyberArk Identity, PAM, EPM; Imprivata SecureLink VPAM
- **Endpoint Security** — CrowdStrike EDR for FDA-regulated medical devices; Absolute endpoint resilience
- **Email Security** — Proofpoint
- **Observation & Intelligence** — Cortex XSIAM (Palo Alto AI-driven SOC/SIEM); CDR
- **Compliance** — HIPAA, FDA device security, zero-trust architecture

Before moving full-time into security architecture, I spent years in enterprise IT at the University of North Texas, where I built open source tooling for Windows endpoint management, Active Directory automation, and infrastructure-as-code — much of which lives on GitHub under my personal account and the [UNT-CAS org](https://github.com/UNT-CAS).

I also completed a Master of Science in Learning Technologies at UNT. Those [course projects and reflections](/masters/) are preserved on this site.

When I'm not thinking about security, I'm probably playing Minecraft with my kids or tinkering with my homelab.
